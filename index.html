Methodology: Revenue per employee = (Revenue in $B × 1,000,000,000) ÷ Employees. Profit per employee uses the same formula with net income (or operating income for segments). Years since founding normalizes across different founding dates.

Inflation Adjustment: When "2025 Dollars" is enabled, all values are adjusted using the BLS CPI-U annual averages (base: 2025). This makes cross-era comparisons meaningful—e.g., Microsoft's 1996 revenue/employee is expressed in today's purchasing power.

Segment Note: Product segments (YouTube, AWS, iPhone, etc.) use their parent company's total employee count as the denominator, yielding an upper bound on per-employee efficiency since the segment doesn't employ all company staff.

Private Companies: Data for OpenAI, Anthropic, SpaceX, xAI, and 23 AI startups are estimates from The Information, Bloomberg, Reuters, Sacra, TechCrunch, Forbes, and analyst reports. Shown with dashed lines.
